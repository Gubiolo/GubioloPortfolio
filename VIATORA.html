VIATORA

Dystopian kit related to pilgrimage ritual

Starting from the ritual of pilgrimage, which we analyzed in its evasive meaning, Viatora speculates on the theme of hyper productivity by creating a fictitious world where the latter is taken to extremes. Viatoraâ€™s movement is a critique of this trend toward super efficiency. The members of the community, called homo viator, want to give back the right importance to the love for themselves and their spaces of reflection through. The movement uses Itinero, the sacred object of the community to atone for daily productivity. After getting rid of the smartphone, symbol of productivity, through a circumambulation experience guided by a sound element, the follower falls into a state of quietness.

Contribution : Speculative design, Interaction design, Art Direction

Team : Arturo Brunetti, Giulia Lisoni, Alice Fuga, Andrea Pancher
