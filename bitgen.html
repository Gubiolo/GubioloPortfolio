BIT GENERATION

A movement against eco villain websites.

Foreword: This project should not be here.
Bit Generation is a project born from scratch in 3 days during a Designalism workshop held at Politecnico di Milano. The installation proposes a speculation on the topic of Internet pollution. Bit Generation is a fictitious group of hackers who insinuate themselves inside the heaviest and most polluting sites by changing their css and with it their structure to bring them to the most ecological form possible using bitmap images and static interactions. The installation included two poster posters, a television on which the video presentation of the movement ran, and a series of reams of sheets with the entire html code of sites we use every day printed on them so as to offer the public a material dimension of the impact of these pages.
