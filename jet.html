JETOLOGY

Sacred journey into the private jets debate

In 2022 private jets have become a relevant topic in the fight against climate change. Using #banprivatejets, groups of activists, politicians and scientists have begun to highlight the need to tax emissions of private jets more decisively or, more extremely, to ban them. The project has been carried out during the Final Synthesis Design Studio held by DensityDesign of the MSc in Communication Design, Politecnico di Milano. The website is an interactive archive that analyzes and breaks down the debate created in the comments section of tiktok videos related to the topic through the use of categorization by keywords. This web project is part of previous research published in a report.

Contribution : Data Visualization/ Web Design/ Creative Coding

Team : Antonio Sacchet, Chen Kangying, Donato Renzulli, Giuseppe Defilippis, Maria Martinuz, Yuying Tang
