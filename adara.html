ADARA

A small artisan brass jewelry business.

This branding for the small artisan business starts from the values of elegance, cleanliness expressed by the products linked to a very friendly and gentle tone of voice of the artisan. Adara is the brightest star in the constellation of the greater dog, starting from this very notion the logo was designed seeking a visual harmony between the product and the idea of star and shine. The star in the logo thus becomes both the shimmer that emphasizes the first letter of the name and the pendant to represent the jewelry. The brand identity was designed accordingly by using elements of the logo combined with graphic imagery.
