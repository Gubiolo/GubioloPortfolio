PIVÓ

A community app for street-basket players

Designing a service for the acceleration of social dynamics and the creation of a solid community around street-basketball as an active asset to enhance the urban context. Pivó is a community app that acts as a digital space to bring together and increase street-basketball fans. This is done through information services about facilities in the area, the organization of games and events, and the multimedia sharing of life on the court, while maintaining the spontaneity and simplicity that is inherent in street sport. Pivo's communication blends the styles of big sports brands dictated by bold fonts and great use of photography with the sparkling colors, rounded shapes and customizable elements typical of social communities. All this told in a direct, ironic and irreverent tone of voice. Communication divides into physical, with the poster system in environments near the basketball courts, and digital with a responsive showcase website for PC, tablets and smartphones.
